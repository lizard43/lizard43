Format notes

300 baud uses the Jay Fenton 300BLOAD rules and decodes to a text/token stream. 2000 baud AstroBASIC tapes are the saved AstroBASIC representation. The converter source shows that 300-baud text is first fed through AstroBASIC's own input routines, then the resulting program may be written at 2000 baud. So 2000-baud tapes can be carrier-decoded here, but some are internal BASIC images or :RUN binary payloads, not plain listings.
