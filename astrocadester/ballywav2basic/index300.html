Format notes

The bit rules are fixed by the Bally 300-baud format: 1 is eight short cycles, 0 is four long cycles, a byte starts with a zero start bit and then eight LSB-first data bits, and the loader syncs on ten consecutive one bits. The options below are only rescue knobs for bad recordings.
